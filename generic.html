     Using Historical materialist analysis Marx explained the contradictions of the capitalist Society That he lived in, the industrial capitalism But also outlined the potential to overcome it He Recognized that "the history of all hitherto exist society is the history of class struggles" And therefore that antagonism had exissted way before industrial capitalism as well as explaining that capitalism Antagonism are devided and polarized into two fundamental classes, The Buorgeoisie and the proletariat

     In 1867 the first volume of das capital or "the capital" was published the only one in Marx's lifetime. It's a revolutionary work that explain in detail contradictions of the capitalist mode of production.   In it marx analyze the worker-capitalist relationship and how the exploitation of the worker is one of the central aspects of the capitalist system.. since the worker don't own the means of production they have no control aver their labor and , therefore are essentially no more than machines working to produce a profit for their capitalist masters who got rich off by not paying the workers a full value of what they produce . Which is the surplus value .... the little difference at which the product is sold and what they pay the worker who made it..

"Capital is dead labour, that vampire like ,only lives by sucking living labour , and lives the more labour it sucks ."

    Since the ultimate goal in the capitalist system is to Maximizes it's profits everything becomes a commodity that is bougth and paid for, Even inpecting basic human needs such as education , healthcare ,housing , food.
Marx Argues that the capitalist system is ultimately unstable because it cannot endlessly increase profits... The unsustainability of this capilist goal of endless profits and consumption can be seen more clearly in our world today.. it causes numerous extinction events , environmental disasters, labor exploitation and leading to the rapid destruction of our planet and everything in it.
