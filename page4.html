Capitalism

     The long reign of feudal aristocracy was opposed by the bourgeoisie, the new class which fnally came to control the trading and manufaturing towns which grew up in the middle ages. The Bourgeoise sparked the industrial revolution meant the new capitalism which exploited the workers. Marx Also in turn believed that capitalism was essential for without capitalism industrialization would have been impossible.But the confident buorgeoisie will not retain their wealth they are doomed to be overthrown by the new working class "the proletariat". Marxist laws say that the capitalist can only pay his workers just enough to keep the alive,these laws also say that the poverty of the proletariat must get worse and worse there is no escape from these cycle.Because of this and other inherent weaknesses capitalism will begin to fall apart.When the historical process is complete but not until then ,the class-concious proletariat will overthrow it's oppressors the bourgeoisie in a final revolution.
